About

Hello! I’m Anne, the heart and hands behind Westside Pet Care, LLC. With a strong sense of responsibility and a deep love for animals, I provide professional overnight pet sitting tailored to your pet’s comfort and needs. Whether you're away for a weekend or an extended trip, I’ll be there to care for your pets as if they were my own.

I'm based in West Atlanta but frequently travel across the country to care for pets in a variety of homes and environments. I have been providing in-home services since 2019, but I've cared for animals of all kinds throughout my life. Dogs, cats, birds, fish, farm animals, horses — these are just a few of the beautiful pets I've had the honor of caring for. I can't wait to meet you and your best friends.

Services

Overnight Pet Sitting – $95–$120/night

Although this is not a comprehensive list of services provided, each overnight stay includes:

  • Morning and evening feedings
  • Walks or potty breaks
  • Medication administration
  • Litter box or cage cleaning
  • Basic grooming like brushing or paw wiping
  • Lots of play, love, and companionship
  • Light home care – mail, packages, plants, etc.

I care for dogs, cats, birds, reptiles, and some farm animals. For custom services like drop-in visits or dog walks, please contact me directly.

Policies

  • Pets must be up to date on vaccinations
  • Let's meet-up before the first stay so we can get to know each other
  • Overnight visits in drug-free and smoke-free homes only
