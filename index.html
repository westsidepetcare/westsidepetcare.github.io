About

I’ve been providing in-home overnight pet sitting services since 2019. Throughout my life, I’ve had the privilege of caring for a wide variety of animals—including dogs, cats, birds, fish, lizards, hamsters, chickens, and horses. I’m based in West Atlanta and often travel throughout the country for pet sitting. I treat your pets and your home with the same care and attention I would give my own. A complimentary meet & greet is required prior to the first stay.

Services

This is not a comprehensive list—just a preview of what can be provided while pet parents are away!

  • Overnight Pet Sitting: $95–$120 per night depending on travel and care needs.
  • Feeding and fresh water
  • Walks and playtime
  • Medication administration
  • Clean-up and litter box maintenance
  • Home security checks
  • Bringing in mail/packages

Please contact me directly to discuss additional services such as drop-in visits and dog walks.

Policies

  • Meet and greet required before first stay
  • Payment for services is due prior to the stay
  • Home must be smoke-free, safe, and have care instructions provided
