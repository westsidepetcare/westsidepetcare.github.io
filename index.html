About

Hi there! I’m Anne, your go-to pet care enthusiast at Westside Pet Care, LLC. As an animal lover who’s been looking after pets for as long as I can remember, I know how important it is to make sure your pets are safe, comfortable, and happy when you're away. I specialize in overnight care that’s tailored to meet each pet’s unique personality and needs.

I'm based in West Atlanta but frequently travel across the country to care for pets in a variety of homes and environments. I’ve been providing in-home services since 2019, but I’ve cared for animals of all kinds throughout my life. Dogs, cats, birds, fish, farm animals, horses—these are just a few of the beautiful pets I've had the honor of caring for. I can’t wait to meet you and your best friends.

Services

Overnight Pet Sitting – Starting at $95/night

Although this is not a comprehensive list of services provided, each overnight stay includes:

  • Morning and evening feedings
  • Walks or potty breaks
  • Medication administration
  • Litter box or cage cleaning
  • Basic grooming like brushing or paw wiping
  • Lots of play, love, and companionship
  • Light home care – mail, packages, plants, etc.

I care for dogs, cats, birds, reptiles, and some farm animals. For custom services like drop-in visits or dog walks, please contact me directly.

Policies

  • Pets should be up to date on vaccinations
  • Let's meet-up before the first stay so we can get to know each other
  • Overnight visits in drug-free and smoke-free homes only
