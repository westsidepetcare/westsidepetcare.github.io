About

Hello! I’m Anne, the heart and hands behind Westside Pet Care. With a strong sense of responsibility and a deep love for animals, I provide professional overnight pet sitting tailored to your pet’s comfort and needs. Whether you're away for a weekend or an extended trip, I’ll be there to care for your pets as if they were my own. I’m based in Atlanta but proudly travel nationwide for select clients.

Services

Overnight Pet Sitting – $120/night
+ $5 per additional animal

Each overnight stay includes:

  • Morning and evening feedings
  • Walks or potty breaks
  • Medication administration
  • Litter box or cage cleaning
  • Basic grooming like brushing or paw wiping
  • Lots of play, love, and companionship
  • Light home care – mail, packages, plants, etc.

I care for dogs, cats, birds, reptiles, and some farm animals.
For custom services like drop-in visits or dog walks, please contact me directly.

Policies

  • Pets must be up to date on vaccinations
  • A meet-and-greet is strongly recommended before the first stay
  • The home must be safe, clean, and have clear instructions provided
  • I do not offer pet boarding in my home at this time
